About

Hi! My name is Amalie and I am a postdoctoral researcher in astrophysics. My primary scientific interest is Galactic archaeology where we learn about the history and evolution of our own galaxy - the Milky Way - by studying the demographics of its stars.

I am current part of the CartographY at University of Birmingham. Here I am working on the use of stellar inference in many different contexts -- especially stellar inference on populations of stars and where we combine the orbits and chemical compositions of stars with ages derived from the study of stellar pulsations known as asteroseismology. The interplay between these observations allows us to make detailed studies of the Milky Way.
